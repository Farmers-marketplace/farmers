Why Choose Farming?

Farming is a rewarding way to connect with nature, grow your own food, and contribute to sustainability.

Steps to Start Farming

  • Step 1: Select the right land.
  • Step 2: Decide what crops or livestock to focus on.
  • Step 3: Prepare the soil and gather resources.
  • Step 4: Start planting or raising livestock.

Farming Tips

- Use organic fertilizers.

- Water plants consistently.

- Rotate crops for better yield.
